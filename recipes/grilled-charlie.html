CHARLIE'S GRILLED CHARLIE

Ingredients list

  • Bread
  • American cheese slice
  • Butter
  • Chocolate spread or syrup
  • Peanut butter

Cooking instructions

  1. Butter one side of a slice of bread, and American cheese slice on the other
  2. Place on hot pan/skillet American chesse side down (yes you read that right)
  3. Apply chocolate spread or syrup on the buttered side
  4. Place another piece of buttered bread on top of the chocolate, butter side down
  5. Spread peanute butter on the top
  6. Flip the whole sandwich, keeping as much cheese as possible
  7. Cook for one last minute
Instructions taken from Binging with Babish
