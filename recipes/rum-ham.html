FRANK'S RUM HAM

Ingredients list

  • Smoked ham fully cooked, bone-in
  • Rum
  • Brown sugar
  • Pineapple
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Cooking instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180C
  2. Score the skin of the smoked ham
  3. Mix brown sugar, salt, and pepper for the dry rub in a bowl
  4. Liberally apply the dry rub all over the smoked ham
  5. Place in preheated 180C oven for 1:30 hours
  6. Caramelise some pineapple chunks over high heat in a frying pan
  7. Add half a cup of rum to the pineapple chunks in the pan
  8. Cook off the alcohol
  9. Take out the ham from the oven
  10. Slice up to serve
  11. Pour pineapple chunks over rum ham slices
Instructions taken from Binging with Babish
